Main Interests

I am mainly interested in the design, formal verification and safety assurance of smart cyber-physical systems. Following are some key areas of my research:

  • SafetyOps
  • safety assurance of autonomous systems (ISO 26262, ISO/PAS 21448, IEC 61508, …)
  • failure and safety analysis of robotics and machine learning algorithms for autonomous systems
  • formal methods assisted testing and runtime verification
  • combined safety and security specification, formal reasoning and verification

Services/Memberships

  • Member: IEC - SEG 11: Future Sustainable Transportation
  • Member: IEC - SEG 10: Ethics in Autonomous and Artificial Intelligence Applications
  • TC Member: IEEE Technical Committee on Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS)
  • Member: CAC/ISO/TC 22 - Road Vehicles, Standards Council of Canada (SCC)

Background

I currently work for the autonomous driving division at Bosch Research and Technology Center (RTC), Silicon Valley Campus. I have previously worked for Blackberry QNX Software Systems, BorgWarner - Waterloo Technical Center, and Magna Electronics (a unit of Magna International Inc). I also worked as a Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council (NSERC) postdoctoral research fellow at McMaster University, Hamilton, ON., Canada.
